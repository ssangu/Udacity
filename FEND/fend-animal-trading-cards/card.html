Cheetah

The cheetah is the fastest land animal in the world, reaching speeds of up to 113km/h. They can accelerate from 0 to 100km/h in just 3 seconds.

  • Scientific name: Acinonyx jubatus
  • Speed: 110 – 120 km/h (In Short Bursts, Running)
  • Mass: 21 – 72 kg (Adult)
  • Height: 66 – 94 cm (Adult, At Shoulder)

Built for speed, cheetahs are slender, with long thin legs and a long tail. They have coarse, short fur that is yellowish tan in colour and covered in solid black spots. Black tear-shaped streaks on the face help to reflect the sun when hunting.
